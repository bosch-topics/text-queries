If prompted, “Login with SSO” using NTID, or you can also create a ticket by navigating from the Employee Self Service portal → User Access Management → OneIDM Support Request.

Steps to Create a Ticket:

  1. Step 1: Click "Request for" and select "Myself or Others".
  2. Step 2: Enter the "Beneficiary NT ID".
  3. Step 3: Select Application as "oneIDM".
  4. Step 4: Select Module as "Others".
  5. Step 5: Enter the "Business reason" in the "Business justification for access".
  6. Step 6: Enter the details about access or issue in "Describe access right / Tcode / Issue".
  7. Step 7: Add any error screenshot as an attachment.
  8. Step 8: Click "Submit", and a ServiceNow ticket will be created.
  9. Step 9: An email notification will be triggered when a ticket is created, and the ticket status can be checked in the ServiceNow portal. Additionally, email notifications will be sent for every update or change to the ticket.
  10. Step 10: To check the status of the created ticket in the ServiceNow portal, kindly click the "Request" option. The created ticket number will be listed there, and the user can check the ticket status.
